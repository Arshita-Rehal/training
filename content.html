About

cloud computing

Cloud computing refers to the delivery of computing services, such as servers, storage, networking, databases, software, and analytics, over the internet (the cloud) to offer faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale. It provides access to resources like storage, databases, servers, networking, software, and analytics on a pay-as-you-go pricing model, which means you only pay for what you use.

keypoints

  • On-demand service: Cloud computing allows users to access computing resources as needed and on demand.
  • Pay-as-you-go: You only pay for the resources you use, similar to a utility billing model.
  • Scalability: The ability to easily scale resources up or down based on demand.

Types of cloud services

  • Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)
  • Platform as a Service (PaaS)
  • as a Service (SaaS) are the primary cloud service models
